    A Convenient Stay Irvine

    Off I-5, I-405, and CA-133 near business and universities

    In the heart of Orange County, La Quinta® by Wyndham Irvine Spectrum provides a comfortable stay off I-5, I-405, and CA-133 with easy access to the University of California Irvine and Concordia University Irvine. If you're here for work, we're a short drive from companies such as Verizon, Google, and General Electric. Plus, family fun awaits with Disneyland® only 15 miles away. You'll also love the uniqueness of our hotel, as it was a former bean and grain warehouse that’s on the National Register of Historic Places.

    Amenities to Relax & Recharge

    Enjoy free breakfast, a fitness center, and outdoor pool

    Our everyday essentials and signature Here for You service shine bright. Wake up with a free Bright Side Breakfast® of eggs, waffles, and more to get energized for your day. Keep connected with free WiFi and squeeze in a workout at the gym. We also offer an outdoor pool where you can enjoy a refreshing swim or a soak in the hot tub. Each of our non-smoking guest rooms features a desk, flat-screen HDTV, coffee maker, and ironing amenities. Plus, you can bring your dog or cat along with our pet-friendly rooms.

    Take It All In

    Explore the outdoors, browse museums, or visit amusement parks

    La Quinta® by Wyndham Irvine Spectrum is close to it all. Spend time outside at one of the nearby parks such as Peters Canyon Regional Park and Limestone Canyon Regional Park. Or, there’s plenty of museums to discover including the Irvine Museum and Lyon Air Museum. Plus, you can easily reach Southern California’s top attractions, from Disneyland® and Knott’s Berry Farm to the beaches of Orange County.
