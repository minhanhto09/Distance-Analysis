    Stay with Us in Tucson/Marana

    Off I-10 close to the University of Arizona and TUS Airport

    Our La Quinta Inn & Suites by Wyndham Tucson - Northwest Marana hotel in South Arizona offers easy access to downtown Tucson and Phoenix via I-10. Located 20 miles north of Tucson International Airport (TUS), we're near local destinations like the University of Arizona, Saguaro National Park, the Tucson Convention Center, and Casino Del Sol as well as a plethora of shopping and dining options. Those here for work can stay productive with our business center and free WiFi. Pet-friendly rooms (available for a fee), an outdoor pool, and fitness center round out a great stay in Northwest Tucson.

    Everything for a Bright Stay

    Enjoy free breakfast and WiFi—plus, a gym and pool

    A bright stay awaits at our Tucson hotel. With amenities like our on-site fitness center, outdoor pool, hot tub, and business center, you'll have everything you need for a great trip. Get energized with a free Bright Side Breakfast® and stay connected to the office and friends with free WiFi. Each of our non-smoking guest rooms features a mini-refrigerator, microwave, desk, ironing amenities, and flat-screen HDTV. Plus, our hotel is pet-friendly (for an extra fee), offers free solar panel parking, and features a relaxing outdoor barbeque area.

    Discover Tucson

    Take in a show, try your luck at a casino, or go shopping

    Enjoy all that Tucson has to offer during your stay. Take in a show at nearby Tucson Arena. Try your luck at Casino del Sol, or enjoy an afternoon of shopping at the Tucson Mall or Park Place. Hit the links at area golf courses, find a view along trails in Saguaro National Park, or experience the Sonoran Desert and learn all about the plants and animals at the Arizona-Sonora Desert Museum.
